Data preprocessing–focused papers (PISA 2018 emphasis)
- Frontiers (2020) – Exploration via data mining: often details missing value handling, categorical encoding, feature importance extraction.
	https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2020.575167/full
- PLOS ONE (2025) – Strategic ML models: likely enumerates feature selection pipeline, algorithm comparison setup, potential treatment (or omission) of plausible values.
	https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0326121
- Educación XX1 (2024) – High reading achievement ML approach: may describe high performer class definition, imbalance handling (e.g., SMOTE or sampling), school vs student variable merging.
	https://www.redalyc.org/journal/706/70681678009/70681678009.pdf
- Neural Computing & Applications (2023) – Science achievement prediction: cross-algorithm comparison suggests standardized preprocessing pipeline (encoding, scaling, train/test split) + hyperparameter tuning protocol.
	https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00521-023-08901-6
- Cities (2022) – Urban vs rural school performance: likely includes aggregation rules from student-level to school-level, exclusion criteria for sparse schools, handling of socioeconomic/context indicators.
	https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0264275122000488
- Journal of Psychoeducational Assessment (2024) – Brazilian proficiency predictors: mixed statistical + ML approaches often outline variable selection (filter vs wrapper), multicollinearity checks.
	https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/07342829241262861
- British Journal of Educational Psychology (2024) – Life satisfaction prediction: subjective well-being scale construction, potential composite score derivation, distribution skew handling.
	https://bpspsychub.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/bjep.12657
- Educational Psychology (2024) – Well-being & math literacy: integration of psychosocial variables, potential normalization or z-score transformations, feature set refinement.
	https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/01443410.2024.2359104
- 추가 검토 포인트: Plausible Values(복수 성취 점수) 사용 여부, sampling weight 적용 여부, 다수준 구조(학교/학생) 처리 방식 명시 여부.
